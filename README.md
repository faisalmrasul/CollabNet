# CollabNet

Earn money by creating content for popular brands! CollabNet is a dedicated platform for content creators in Bangladesh to discover and work on paid campaigns with well-known local brands.

## What Is CollabNet?

CollabNet is a specialized job board for content creators. Browse paid opportunities from top brands looking for creators to promote their products on social media.

### How It Works

1. **Browse Campaigns**
   Explore available campaigns and choose those that fit your style. Each listing includes:
   - Clear payment details
   - Content type required (video, image post, or text with photos)
   - Specific submission deadlines

2. **Create with AI Assistance**
   Once you accept a campaign, use our built-in AI tools to help you:
   - Write catchy headlines
   - Generate video scripts
   - Create relevant hashtags
   - Jumpstart your creative process

3. **Submit & Track Performance**
   After submitting your content, the app tracks key metrics:
   - Reach and impressions
   - Engagement rates
   Your earnings grow based on content performance. Hit your targets to unlock bonus payments on top of your base rate.

## Features

- **Localized Experience:** Fully in Bengali, making it accessible for creators across Bangladesh
- **Transparent Payments:** Clear rates and performance-based bonuses
- **AI-Powered Creativity:** Built-in tools to assist with content creation
- **Earnings Dashboard:** Track your income and campaign performance in one place

## Why Choose CollabNet?

CollabNet combines a personal earnings dashboard with a creative assistant, designed specifically to help Bangladeshi content creators grow their income through brand collaborations.
